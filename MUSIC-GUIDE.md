## Option 1: Free Royalty-Free Music Sites

Download a Happy Birthday tune from these sites (all free and legal):

1. **YouTube Audio Library** (Requires Google account)
   - Visit: https://studio.youtube.com/channel/UC/music
   - Search for "birthday" or "celebration"
   - Download as MP3

2. **Free Music Archive**
   - Visit: https://freemusicarchive.org/
   - Search: "happy birthday" or "celebration"
   - Filter by "Commercial use allowed"

3. **Pixabay Music**
   - Visit: https://pixabay.com/music/
   - Search: "birthday" or "happy celebration"
   - All royalty-free!

4. **Bensound**
   - Visit: https://www.bensound.com/
   - Check out "Happy Rock" or "Birthday" tracks

## Option 2: Use Your Own Song

If you have a special song that reminds you of Adi:
- Just make sure it's in MP3 format
- Any music player can convert other formats to MP3

## How to Add the Music

Once you have your MP3 file:

1. **Rename it to:** `birthday-music.mp3`
2. **Place it in** the same folder as `index.html`
3. **That's it!** The website will automatically play it when the music button is clicked
