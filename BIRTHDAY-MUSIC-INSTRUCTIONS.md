## What You Need

A Happy Birthday song in **MP3 format**

## How to Add It

1. **Download** or get your Happy Birthday MP3 file
2. **Rename it** to: `birthday-music.mp3`
3. **Place it** in the same folder as `index.html`

## Where to Get Happy Birthday Music (Free)

### Option 1: Royalty-Free Sites
- **Pixabay Music**: https://pixabay.com/music/search/happy%20birthday/
- **Bensound**: https://www.bensound.com/
- **Free Music Archive**: https://freemusicarchive.org/

### Option 2: YouTube to MP3 (Personal Use Only)
1. Find a Happy Birthday song on YouTube
2. Use a YouTube to MP3 converter (like ytmp3.cc)
3. Download and rename to `birthday-music.mp3`

## When Will It Play?

The music will automatically start playing:
- **At 12:00 AM IST on January 16, 2026**
- When the countdown reaches zero
- It will loop in the background during the cake candle-blowing
- And continue playing as he reads your letter and views photos

## Testing It

You can test the music by:
1. Opening the browser console (F12)
2. Running: `document.getElementById('birthday-music').play()`
